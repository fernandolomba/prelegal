# Software License Agreement

A comprehensive agreement governing the licensing of software to customers, including installation rights, updates, payment, and confidentiality.

Key sections:
1. **Software** — License grant, user accounts, feedback, machine learning, open source, updates, reservation of rights.
2. **Restrictions & Obligations** — Customer restrictions, suspension rights.
3. **Payment & Taxes** — Fees, invoicing, automatic payment, taxes, payment disputes.
4. **Term & Termination** — Order form, framework terms, termination rights, force majeure, effect of termination, survival.
5. **Representations & Warranties** — Mutual, provider warranty, warranty exclusions, warranty remedy.
6. **Disclaimer of Warranties** — AS IS disclaimer.
7. **Limitation of Liability** — Liability caps, damages waiver, exceptions.
8. **Indemnification** — Provider and customer protection, procedure, changes to product.
9. **Confidentiality** — Non-use, non-disclosure, exclusions, permitted disclosures.
10. **General Terms** — Entire agreement, governing law, beta products, logo rights, export controls, anti-bribery.
11. **Definitions** — Full glossary including Affiliate, Beta Product, Confidential Information, Open Source Software, Updates, Usage Data.
