# AI Addendum

1. <span class="header_2" id="1">AI Services</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="1.1">Using AI Services.</span>  The AI Services are part of the Product and subject to the Agreement as supplemented by this AI Addendum. Customer may use AI Services by providing Input. The AI Services may generate Output in response to Input.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="1.2">Restrictions.</span>  Customer will not: (a) use the AI Services for decision-making in a regulated industry without proper human oversight; (b) use the AI Services to violate intellectual property rights of others; or (c) falsely state Output was created by a human.
    3. <span class="header_3" id="1.3">Model Training.</span>  Unless the Cover Page identifies Training Data and Training Purposes, Provider may not use Customer's Inputs or Outputs to Train any Model.
    4. <span class="header_3" id="1.4">Non-Training Improvement.</span>  Provider may use Input, Output, and Training Data to provide, maintain, develop, and improve the AI System, provided such usage does not constitute Training except as authorized.
2. <span class="header_2" id="2">Intellectual Property and Privacy</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="2.1">Ownership.</span>  Customer retains all right, title, and interest in Input, and owns all Output. Provider assigns to Customer all right, title, and interest in Output to the extent permitted by Applicable Laws.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="2.2">Personal Data.</span>  Nothing in this AI Addendum will reduce Provider's obligations under Applicable Data Protection Laws regarding Personal Data in Input.
    3. <span class="header_3" id="2.3">Rights to Input.</span>  Customer represents and warrants that it has all rights necessary to submit Input to the AI Services.
3. <span class="header_2" id="3">Disclaimers</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="3.1">Nature of AI.</span>  Information generated by the AI Services may be incorrect or inaccurate. The AI Services are not human and are not a substitute for human oversight. Output may not be protectable as intellectual property.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="3.2">Similarity of Output.</span>  Output may resemble or be duplicative of materials created for others. Provider does not warrant that Output will not incorporate data of others or infringe intellectual property rights.
4. <span class="header_2" id="4">Definitions</span>
    1. **"AI Services"** means the artificial intelligence or machine learning components of the Product.
    2. **"AI System"** means the AI application and services layers, excluding underlying Models.
    3. **"Input"** means data, information, prompts, or materials submitted to the AI Services, excluding Feedback.
    4. **"Model"** means a large language, machine learning, or artificial intelligence model.
    5. **"Output"** means data, information, or materials created by the AI Services in response to Input.
    6. **"Train"** or **"Training"** means the use of data to create or improve a Model.
