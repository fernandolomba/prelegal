# Professional Services Agreement

A comprehensive agreement governing the provision of professional services between a Provider and Customer, including deliverables, intellectual property assignment, payment, and confidentiality.

Key sections:
1. **Services** — Providing services, cooperation, change orders, acceptance, subcontractors, customer obligations.
2. **Intellectual Property** — Deliverables ownership, customer materials, pre-existing materials, third-party materials, feedback.
3. **Privacy & Security** — Personal data, security policy.
4. **Payment & Taxes** — Fees, invoicing, taxes, payment disputes.
5. **Term & Termination** — Term, termination rights, effect of termination, survival.
6. **Representations & Warranties** — Mutual, customer, provider warranties and remedies.
7. **Disclaimer of Warranties** — AS IS disclaimer for non-warranted items.
8. **Limitation of Liability** — Liability caps, damages waiver, exceptions.
9. **Indemnification** — Provider and customer protection, procedure.
10. **Insurance** — Coverage requirements during and after term.
11. **Confidentiality** — Non-use, non-disclosure, exclusions, permitted disclosures.
12. **General Terms** — Entire agreement, governing law, assignment, anti-bribery.
13. **Definitions** — Full glossary including Affiliate, Change Order, Customer Materials, Deliverables, Pre-Existing Materials, SOW.
