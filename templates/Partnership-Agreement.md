# Partnership Agreement

A standard partnership agreement governing cooperation between two companies including trademark licensing, payment, confidentiality, and indemnification.

Key sections:
1. **Cooperation** — Obligations, feedback.
2. **Payment & Taxes** — Fees, billing, payment, taxes (when applicable).
3. **Trademark License** — License grant, reservation of rights, restrictions, approval rights.
4. **Privacy** — DPA reference if applicable.
5. **Escalation Procedure** — Dispute resolution before legal action.
6. **Term & Termination** — Term, termination rights, force majeure, effect of termination, survival.
7. **Representations & Warranties** — Mutual warranties including IP non-infringement.
8. **Disclaimer of Warranties** — AS IS disclaimer.
9. **Limitation of Liability** — Liability caps, damages waiver, exceptions.
10. **Indemnification** — Company and partner protection, procedure.
11. **Confidentiality** — Non-use, non-disclosure, exclusions, permitted disclosures.
12. **General Terms** — Entire agreement, governing law, assignment, anti-bribery.
13. **Definitions** — Full glossary including Affiliate, Brand Elements, Covered Claim, Licensor, Licensee.
