# Data Processing Agreement

A comprehensive data processing agreement governing the processing of personal data by a Provider on behalf of a Customer, including GDPR compliance, SCCs, and security incident response.

Key sections:
1. **Processor and Subprocessor Relationships** — Provider as Processor or Subprocessor depending on Customer's role.
2. **Processing** — Processing details, instructions, subprocessors, consent to processing.
3. **Restricted Transfers** — Authorization, Ex-EEA transfers (EEA SCCs), Ex-UK transfers (UK Addendum), other international transfers.
4. **Security Incident Response** — Notification within 72 hours, response steps.
5. **Audit & Reports** — Audit rights, security reports, due diligence.
6. **Coordination & Cooperation** — Response to inquiries, DPIAs and DTIAs.
7. **Deletion of Customer Personal Data** — Deletion by customer, deletion at DPA expiration.
8. **Limitation of Liability** — Subject to Agreement's waivers and limitations.
9. **Conflicts Between Documents** — EEA SCCs > DPA > Agreement.
10. **Term of Agreement** — Continues until Agreement expires or is terminated.
11. **Definitions** — Full glossary including Applicable Laws, Controller, Customer Personal Data, EEA SCCs, GDPR, Processing, Processor, Security Incident, Subprocessor, UK GDPR, UK Addendum.
