# Pilot Agreement

A standard agreement for providing pilot or trial access to a product for evaluation purposes, prior to entering a definitive long-term agreement.

Key sections:
1. **Pilot Access** — Access and use for evaluation purposes, license for software, user accounts, customer content, feedback, restrictions, reservation of rights.
2. **Term & Termination** — Starts on Effective Date through Pilot Period; either party may terminate for any reason on 30 days notice; effect of termination includes deletion of software and customer content.
3. **Representations** — Mutual representations of legal power and authority.
4. **Disclaimer of Warranties** — Product provided AS IS; all warranties disclaimed.
5. **Limitation of Liability** — General liability cap per Order Form; damages waiver except for confidentiality breaches.
6. **Confidentiality** — Non-use, non-disclosure, exclusions, permitted disclosures.
7. **General Terms** — Entire agreement, governing law, assignment, taxes, force majeure.
8. **Definitions** — Full glossary including Agreement, Confidential Information, Customer Content, Definitive Agreement, Evaluation Purposes, Feedback, High Risk Activity, Software, User.
