# Business Associate Agreement

1. <span class="header_2">Business Associate Obligations</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Obligations and Restrictions.</span>  Provider may not use or disclose PHI other than as described in this BAA, as permitted under the Privacy Rule, or as otherwise required by applicable law.
    2. <span class="header_3">Permitted Uses and Disclosures.</span>  Provider may only use or disclose PHI as reasonably necessary to provide the Services or as otherwise required by applicable law.
    3. <span class="header_3">Privacy and Information Security Program.</span>  Provider will maintain a privacy and information security program including workforce training, policies, and Privacy and Security Officials as required under HIPAA.
    4. <span class="header_3">Safeguards.</span>  Provider will implement appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of PHI.
    5. <span class="header_3">Subcontractors.</span>  Provider may disclose PHI to a Subcontractor only after ensuring the Subcontractor executes a binding written agreement with protections no less stringent than this BAA.
    6. <span class="header_3">Individual Requests.</span>  Provider will support Company in completing requests related to individuals' rights under HIPAA within ten business days.
2. <span class="header_2">Company Obligations</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Notice of Privacy Practices.</span>  Company will provide Provider with its current notice of privacy practices and notify Provider of any changes that impact Provider's use of PHI.
    2. <span class="header_3">Compliance with Laws.</span>  Company will only use and disclose PHI to Provider in accordance with HIPAA and applicable law.
3. <span class="header_2">Data Rights & Restrictions</span>
    1. Provider is permitted, unless restricted by applicable Limitations, to use PHI outside the United States, de-identify PHI, and aggregate PHI for its own purposes.
4. <span class="header_2">Breach Notification</span>
    1. Provider will report to Company within the Breach Notification Period each unauthorized use or disclosure of PHI, including breaches of unsecured PHI and Security Incidents.
5. <span class="header_2">Term & Termination</span>
    1. This BAA continues until the Agreement ends. Upon termination, Provider will return or destroy all PHI as directed by Company.
6. <span class="header_2">Definitions</span>
    1. Standard definitions including BAA, Breach, Business Associate, Covered Entity, HHS, HIPAA, PHI, Privacy Rule, Security Incident, Security Rule, Services, Subcontractor.
