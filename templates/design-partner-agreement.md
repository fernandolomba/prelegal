# Design Partner Agreement

1. <span class="header_2">Design Partner Overview</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Product Access.</span>  During the Term, Partner will have early access to the Product for its internal business purposes and to give Feedback to Provider and participate in the Program.
    2. <span class="header_3">Program and Feedback.</span>  The purpose of the Program is for Provider to develop, build, and improve the Product. Partner will give Feedback to Provider on a mutually agreed schedule.
    3. <span class="header_3">Product Improvement.</span>  Provider will develop and improve the Product and may use all Feedback freely without any restriction or obligation.
2. <span class="header_2">Fees and Costs</span>
    1. Partner will pay Provider the Fees, if any.
3. <span class="header_2">Term & Termination</span>
    1. <span class="header_3">Agreement Term.</span>  This Agreement will start on the Effective Date and continue for the Term.
    2. <span class="header_3">Termination.</span>  Either party may terminate this Agreement for any or no reason upon 30 days advance notice.
    3. <span class="header_3">Effect of Termination.</span>  Upon expiration or termination: (a) Partner will no longer have any right to access or use the Product; (b) Each Recipient will return or destroy Discloser's Confidential Information.
4. <span class="header_2">Disclaimer of Warranties</span>
    1. Provider and Partner each disclaim all warranties, whether express or implied, including the implied warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, title, and non-infringement.
5. <span class="header_2">Confidentiality</span>
    1. Recipient will only use Discloser's Confidential Information to fulfill its obligations or exercise its rights under this Agreement, and will not disclose it to anyone else.
6. <span class="header_2">Intellectual Property</span>
    1. Provider retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Product. Provider owns all Feedback.
7. <span class="header_2">General Terms</span>
    1. Standard general terms including entire agreement, governing law, injunctive relief, restrictions, assignment, notices, independent contractors, and signature provisions.
8. <span class="header_2">Definitions</span>
    1. Standard definitions for Agreement, Applicable Laws, Confidential Information, Cover Page, Discloser, Feedback, Product, and Recipient.
