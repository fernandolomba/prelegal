# Cloud Service Agreement

A comprehensive agreement governing the provision of cloud-based software services between a Provider and Customer. Covers access and use rights, restrictions, privacy and security, payment, term and termination, warranties, liability limitations, indemnification, and confidentiality.

Key sections:
1. **Service** — Access and use, support, user accounts, feedback, customer content, machine learning.
2. **Restrictions & Obligations** — Customer restrictions, suspension rights.
3. **Privacy & Security** — Personal data, prohibited data.
4. **Payment & Taxes** — Fees, invoicing, automatic payment, taxes, payment disputes.
5. **Term & Termination** — Order form, framework terms, termination rights, force majeure, survival.
6. **Representations & Warranties** — Mutual, customer, provider warranties and remedies.
7. **Disclaimer of Warranties** — AS IS disclaimer.
8. **Limitation of Liability** — Liability caps, damages waiver, exceptions.
9. **Indemnification** — Provider and customer protection, procedure, exclusions.
10. **Confidentiality** — Non-use, non-disclosure, exclusions, permitted disclosures.
11. **Reservation of Rights** — IP ownership.
12. **General Terms** — Entire agreement, governing law, assignment, notices, anti-bribery.
13. **Definitions** — Full glossary of defined terms.
