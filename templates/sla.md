# Service Level Agreement

1. <span class="header_2" id="1">Uptime</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="1.1">Target Uptime.</span>  If there is a Target Uptime, Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Cloud Service available for at least the Target Uptime as calculated each calendar month.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="1.2">Calculating Uptime.</span>  Provider and Customer agree to calculate availability as the total number of Available Minutes minus Downtime Minutes, divided by total Available Minutes, measured in a calendar month.

2. <span class="header_2" id="2">Response Time</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="2.1">Target Response Time.</span> If there is a Target Response Time, Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to respond to support requests within the Target Response Time.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="2.2">Calculating Response Time.</span>  Response time is calculated from when Customer submits a support request to when Provider specifically acknowledges it. An automated response is not a specific acknowledgement.

3. <span class="header_2" id="3">Remedies</span>
    1. <span class="header_3" id="3.1">Service Credit.</span>  If availability falls below Target Uptime or response time exceeds Target Response Time, Customer is eligible to receive the applicable Service Credit. Service Credits only apply towards future Cloud Service Fees.
    2. <span class="header_3" id="3.2">Requesting A Service Credit.</span>  To receive a Service Credit, Customer must notify Provider within 7 days of the end of the month in which the Service Credit was earned.
    3. <span class="header_3" id="3.3">Service Credit Limitations.</span>  Service Credits may not be exchanged for monetary amounts, do not earn interest, and will not accumulate above 8% of Cloud Service Fees for a Subscription Period.
    4. <span class="header_3" id="3.4">Termination.</span>  If the Cloud Service does not meet Target Uptime for two out of any three consecutive months, Customer may immediately terminate the affected Order Form.
    5. <span class="header_3" id="3.5">Exclusive Remedy.</span>  This SLA describes Customer's exclusive remedy and Provider's entire liability for any failure to meet Target Uptime or Target Response Time.

4. <span class="header_2" id="4">Definitions</span>
    1. **"Available Minutes"** means total minutes in a calendar month, minus Excluded Minutes and Scheduled Downtime.
    2. **"Downtime Minutes"** means total minutes when the Cloud Service is not available, minus Excluded Minutes and Scheduled Downtime.
    3. **"Excluded Minutes"** means unavailability due to Force Majeure, general Internet issues, third-party equipment, or Customer misuse.
    4. **"Service Credit"** means accrued Uptime Credit plus accrued Response Time Credit.
    5. **"SLA Standard Terms"** means these Common Paper Service Level Agreement Standard Terms Version 2.0.
